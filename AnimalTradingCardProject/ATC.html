Siberian Husky

Siberian Husky sled dogs heroically brought lifesaving serum to fight a diphtheria epidemic in Nome, Alaska

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupis
  • Average height: 20-24in
  • Lifespan: 12-14 years
  • Orgin: Siberia

Siberian Huskies originated in Northeast Asia where they were bred by the chukchi people of Siberia for sled pulling and companionship. They are an active, energetic, resilient breed. These days Huskies are kept as house pets but they are still used as sled dogs by competitive and recreational mushers.
